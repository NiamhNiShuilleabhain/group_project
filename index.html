Camouflage
A research about animals which can camouflage and unnoticeable objects in our everyday life. I have created the final piece of art using mixed media and collage. It portrays the unknown, the undercover element of life through the illustration of butterflies coming to life after they have been hidden in their cocoon. The use of textured wooden panels instead of canvas adds a more natural sense to the artwork and emphasises that some things which are present and visible, can remain hidden or unnoticeable.
